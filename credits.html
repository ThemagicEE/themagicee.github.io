Why does this Website exist?

I created this website after building my first Pwnagotchi. I had lots of trouble getting mine to work because many resources are outdated. I had to dig through Discord servers and old guides to figure things out. That's why I created this guide to help anyone who wants to build one themselves. I wanted to make an easy-to-follow guide, and I really hope it helps someone build their own Pwnagotchi.
